### The "Detox-Retox" Framework

Most people think of IV therapy as something you do when you're already feeling terrible. That's backward. The smartest bachelorette groups in Cabo use what I call the "detox-retox" framework, and it changes how the whole trip feels.

Here's how it works. Day one, the group lands in Cabo, tired from travel and slightly dehydrated from the flight. We schedule an arrival IV session at the villa before the first big night out. Everyone gets a Hydration or Myers Cocktail drip, which reloads vitamins, minerals, and fluids that air travel strips away. The group feels sharp, energized, and ready. That's the "detox."

Then the party happens. Night one. Night two. Beach clubs, rooftop bars, dancing, whatever the bride wants.

Then the "retox" kicks in. The morning after the biggest night, I show up at the villa with the Hangover Recovery drips. Saline, B-complex vitamins, anti-nausea medication, pain relief. The whole group is sitting poolside with IVs in their arms, laughing about the night before, feeling human again within the hour. The afternoon is saved.

That cycle, arrival recovery followed by smart morning-after treatment, is the difference between a bachelorette trip that uses up two of its four days and one that fires on all cylinders from arrival to departure.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Group IV Therapy in Cabo San Lucas

### How far in advance should I book a group IV session?

Booking 24 to 48 hours ahead is ideal for most group sessions. For larger groups of 8 or more, or for high-demand dates like holiday weekends and peak season, booking three to five days out gives the best availability. The earlier you lock in a time, the better, especially for wedding-morning sessions where timing is tight.

### What's included in a group IV session, and what does it cost?

Each session includes the IV drip formula of your choice, all clinical supplies, and on-site administration by a licensed RN. Pricing varies by formula and group size. [see current pricing and packages](/contact) The best way to get an accurate quote is to contact Liquid Lounge directly with your group size and preferred formula.

### Is IV therapy safe for everyone in the group?

IV therapy is safe for most healthy adults. A brief health intake is completed before each session to screen for contraindications, including kidney conditions, certain heart conditions, and specific medication interactions. Anyone with a known medical condition should consult their physician before booking. As a licensed RN, I review every guest's intake individually and will not administer a drip if there's a clinical reason not to.

### Can we mix formulas within the same group session?

Yes, absolutely. Different people in the group can choose different formulas based on what they need. One person might want a Hangover Recovery while another wants a Myers Cocktail. That's completely normal and easy to accommodate. Just let me know your group's preferences when you book.

### Where in Los Cabos do you provide mobile IV service?

I serve the full Los Cabos corridor, including Cabo San Lucas, San Jose del Cabo, Pedregal, Palmilla, Querencia, and everywhere in between. If you're in a villa, hotel, resort, or rental property within the Los Cabos area, I can come to you. For locations outside the main corridor, reach out and we'll work it out.
