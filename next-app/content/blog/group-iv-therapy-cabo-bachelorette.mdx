## Frequently Asked Questions About Group IV Therapy in Cabo San Lucas

### How far in advance should I book a group IV session?

We recommend reserving your wellness session as soon as your travel itinerary is confirmed. This ensures your preferred date and time are secured, particularly for wedding mornings, large groups, holiday weekends, and peak travel seasons.

For most groups, booking at least one week in advance provides the greatest flexibility. Wedding parties, corporate retreats, and larger events are encouraged to reserve two to four weeks in advance whenever possible.

While we frequently accommodate last-minute and same-day requests, availability cannot always be guaranteed. If your plans change or you're already in Los Cabos, we'll always do our best to fit you into the schedule.

### What's included in a group IV session, and what does it cost?

Each session includes the IV drip formula of your choice, all clinical supplies, and on-site administration by a licensed professional. Pricing varies by formula and group size. [See current pricing and packages](/contact) — the best way to get an accurate quote is to contact Liquid Lounge directly with your group size and preferred formula.

### Is IV therapy safe for everyone in the group?

Your safety is our highest priority. Before every wellness session, you'll complete a brief medical intake and health assessment to help determine whether IV therapy is appropriate for you.

Your health history, current medications, allergies, and any underlying medical conditions are carefully reviewed before treatment. Certain conditions including kidney disease, specific heart conditions, pregnancy, or potential medication interactions may affect your eligibility or require additional medical clearance.

Every assessment is reviewed by a licensed professional, and all treatments are provided within established medical protocols and under physician oversight. If IV therapy isn't clinically appropriate, we'll explain why and recommend the safest course of action.

### Can everyone choose a different IV treatment?

Absolutely. Every guest is individually assessed and can choose the wellness treatment that best fits their goals and needs.

Some guests may prefer travel recovery with hydration, electrolytes, vitamins, and minerals, while others may choose premium therapies such as NAD+, glutathione, or other wellness enhancements from our signature menu. Every treatment is personalized, making it easy for everyone in your group to receive the care that's right for them, all during the same session.

If your group has specific preferences or wellness goals, simply let us know when booking, and we'll tailor the experience accordingly.

### Where in Los Cabos do you provide mobile IV service?

Liquid Lounge provides concierge mobile wellness services throughout the Los Cabos region, including Cabo San Lucas, San José del Cabo, the Tourist Corridor, Pedregal, Palmilla, Querencia, Puerto Los Cabos, Diamante, El Dorado, Chileno Bay, and surrounding communities.
